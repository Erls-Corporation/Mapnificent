Watch out: Alpha Stage

This software is far from finished and still lacks lots of data.
You should still play around and try the following:

  • change the time distance of point by using the slider
  • add more points by clicking on the map
  • drag points around
  • use the beautiful "Colored" option
  • check "Intersect" (Firefox, Opera only) to see intersections
